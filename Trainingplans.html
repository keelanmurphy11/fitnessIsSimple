Training Plans

Basic Weightlifting Plans

2x/week:

With just 1 hour a week, doing two 30-minute full body sessions you can make huge amounts of progress. People often say they don't have time to train but all you really need is an hour a week.

Exercise (Beginner / Intermediate) Reps Sets
Leg Press / Barbell Squat 8-12 / 5-10 2-3
Hamstring Curl / RDL 8-12 / 5-10 2-3
Incline Machine Press / Incline Dumbbell Press 8-12 / 5-10 2-3
Cable Row / Barbell Row 8-12 / 5-10 2-3
Back Extension / Leg Raises 8-12 2-3

4x/week: Upper/Lower Split

Day 1 & 3: Upper Body

Exercise (Beginner / Intermediate) Reps Sets
Incline machine chest press / Incline dumbbell chest press 8-12 / 5-10 2-3
Cable Row / Barbell Row 8-12 / 5-10 2-3
Dumbbell lateral raise 8-12 2-3
Lat pulldown / Pull-ups 8-12 / 5-10 2-3
Bicep curls 8-12 2-3
Tricep extensions 8-12 2-3

Day 2 & 4: Lower Body

Exercise (Beginner / Intermediate) Reps Sets
Leg Press / Barbell Squat 8-12 / 5-10 2-3
Hamstring Curl / RDL 8-12 / 5-10 2-3
Machine hip thrust / Barbell hip thrust 8-12 / 5-10 2-3
Dumbbell lunges / Barbell lunges 8-12 / 5-10 2-3
Calf raises 8-12 2-3

6x/week: Push/Pull/Legs Split

This split is for pretty advanced lifters or for if you're really serious about building as much muscle as possible. The 6 day split is broken down like so: Day 1 & 4 working chest, shoulders and triceps, days 2 & 5 working back and biceps, and days 3 & 6 working legs.

Day 1 & 4: Push

Exercise (Beginner / Intermediate) Reps Sets
Incline Machine Press / Incline Dumbbell Press 8-12 / 5-10 2-3
Machine shoulder press / Barbell shoulder press 8-12 / 5-10 2-3
Dumbell lateral raises/ Cable lateral raises 8-12 2-3
Machine pec dec / Cable flies 8-12 2-3
Rope tricep extensions / Tricep skullcrushers 8-12 2-3

Day 2 & 5: Pull

Exercise (Beginner / Intermediate) Reps Sets
Lat pulldown / Weighted pull-up 8-12 / 5-10 2-3
Machine row / Barbell row 8-12 / 5-10 2-3
Cable pullover 8-12 2-3
Face pulls / Rear delt flies 8-12 2-3
Bicep curls 8-12 2-3

Day 3 & 6: Legs

Exercise (Beginner / Intermediate) Reps Sets
Leg Press / Barbell Squat 8-12 / 5-10 2-3
Hamstring Curl / RDL 8-12 / 5-10 2-3
Machine hip thrust / Barbell hip thrust 8-12 / 5-10 2-3
